Kitchen Vertical Farm

Macsxperts Kitchen Vertical Farm offers a hassle free automated solution to grow leafy greens and vegetables in the comfort of your balcony/rooftops. Grow and live around green spaces, without having to worry about daily care of the plants.

Urban Farming

One solution to address multiple urban problems of lack of greenery, low water and space availability, lack of time for gardening and lack of fresh crunchy zero pesticide leafy greens.

Sustainable Agriculture

90% less water & land comparatively. Fresh home grown nutritious greens leads to low carbon footprint.

Plug and Play

Includes 6ft tower with 70ltr tank, Pump with timer, Net-pots, Clay pellets, Sample nutrients.

Vertical Farming

Environment Friendly

Food grade quality materials with premium finish.

Automation

Zero hassle. Add water once a week only!
