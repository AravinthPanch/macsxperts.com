Macsxperts

Macsxperts offers a complete solution to growing plants and vegetables in the comfort of your home or on a rooftop. Our goal is to increase green spaces, without having to worry about taking care of the plants. We are also promoting the benefits of growing our own vegetables in the available spaces in our homes and on rooftops.

What can be grown?

Leafy vegetables : Palak, Coriander, Amaranth, Fenugreek Leaves, Lettuce
Herbs : Pudina, Dill, Tulsi, Chives, Sage, Oregano, Rosemary
Vegetables / Fruits / Flowers : Tomatoes, Cucumbers, Cauliflower, Capsicum and Strawberries
More Info

Where can be grown?

Balconies, Rooftops, Walkways, Terrace Garden, Greenhouses, Polyhouses, Indoor Farms, Corporate, Schools and Hospital Green Spaces
More Info

How much effort?

15 mins every week, 30 mins monthly for making seedlings
